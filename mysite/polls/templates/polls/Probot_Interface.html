ABOUT ME






Hi, my name is Probot. I am a chatbot. A chatbot is a computer program which conducts a conversation via auditory or textual methods. I am institute oriented. I provide all information required by you students about the institute, for example, whom to approach for doing a certain project in the campus, office hours of faculty, time-tables, course curriculum, courses available in a semester, medical facilities, etc. I also know about various details and fact about your institute, and I am a guide to many people who wish to know about your institute. Working: I have been made on a Django (Python) framework. My front-end has been developed using HTML, CSS, JS and some Python codes. My back-end also involves some Machine learning algorithms in Python. My database is a csv file which is obtained by web-scraping the required content from the IITGN website (www.iitgn.ac.in).
